A place to arrive

Above the rhythm of Fourth Avenue, a quieter register — light, warm stone, and long views that let the city fall away.

Solace is a collection of thoughtfully composed residences at the corner of St Marks Place, wrapped in warm brick and set over a full floor of amenities. Rooms are calm and unhurried; materials are honest; and every shared space is designed for the way a day actually unfolds — from an early workout to a rooftop evening.
